Why do I think hackers are necessary? By David Valencia

Although most people generally agree that hackers are a type of people that are trying to get your data related to your bank account and among other things, few would deny hacker are a threat for society just because of a few criminal groups. I will be looking at hackers as an essential part of the internet.

To begin with, hackers are divided into three categories as follows: black hat, grey hat, and white hat. As a matter of fact, grey hat hackers are considered to be a huge community connected to global security, since they spend the night looking for breaches on the internet. Moreover, the purposes of grey hat hackers for years have been to break into a well-known website and come up with methods with which one is able to steal a database from the website and it appears to be the best way to improve security on the internet.

The thing is that grey hat hackers are thought to be a solution to issues such as insecurity on the internet. Despite the fact that people consider them a part of the internet to be scared of, if it were not for them, internet insecurity would be a principal point of potential breach for fraudsters. In addition to that, hackers have been developing the internet little by little. Furthermore, some platforms are emerging on the internet to provide jobs to grey hat hackers, because the internet appear to have been improving due to grey hat hackers, they provide solutions that companies hadn’t thought before, and make them earn money.

The Internet would surely be a secure place to navigate on if people with deep knowledge with respect to computer science were given a significant role to play in the field of internet, since in the future they will be providing security to society. Nevertheless, one of the benefits is that owing to hackers the internet has started a period of improvements and privacy rights.
